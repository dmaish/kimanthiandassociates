[ ABOUT US ]

committed to providing personalized and effective legal counsel

Kimanthi and Associates Advocates is a law firm established in 2005 by the senior partner, Musyoki Kimanthi. It is a Mid-sized law firm in Westlands, Nairobi with a team that prides itself in offering quality legal services to it’s clients.

Our practice does encapsulate a vast array of legal services, but we particularly pride ourselves with matters related to Constitutional petitions, personal injury claims, family law, conveyancing & real estate, commercial transactions, banking, and finance law.

[ OUR PRACTICE AREAS ]

committed to providing personalized and effective legal counsel

Constitutional Petitions

Matters stemming from exercising your constitutional right to petition Parliament with regard to any legislations.

Corporate & Commercial Transactions

The registration of businesses, drafting commercial agreements, mergers, acquisitions, and much more.

Personal Injury Claims

Issues arising from any physical injury occasioned by an accident or any other negligent act.

Banking and Finance Law

This involves matters that stem from any compliance issues with banking and governmental bodies, securing funding and borrower or lender interests in said transactions.

Family Law

This involves any matters arising from divorce, child custody, or separation cases and any other matters involving family dispute.

Employment and Labour

Matters arising from employment issues whether that be by employer or employee and related matters.

Conveyancing & Real Estate

Matters stemming from land transactions and securing our client’s interests in said transactions and any third-party interests.

Probate and Administration

We handle matters that involve the drafting of wills, estate management, and related services.
